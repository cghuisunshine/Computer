Grade 7 Friendly Lesson 2

AI Training Lab

Teach a computer a simple pattern by changing weight and bias. Your goal is to make the model's error smaller.

Big idea AI training means improving guesses from examples.
Main formula prediction = weight × input + bias
Your mission Find numbers that make the total error small.

1. Quick Review

In the first lesson, you learned that AI is a pattern-finding machine. It uses examples to learn a rule.

1Guess
2Measure Error
3Adjust Numbers
4Try Again

Training does not mean the computer magically understands. It means the computer keeps adjusting numbers until the predictions get better.

5. Check Your Understanding

Answer these questions after you finish the training labs.

1. What does AI training mean?

2. In prediction = weight × input + bias, what does the weight do?

3. If the real answer is 80 and the prediction is 72, what is the error?

4. Fill in the blank: Good training usually makes the error get ________.

Final Summary

AI training is a cycle: make a prediction, compare it with the real answer, measure the error, and adjust the model. In this lesson, you trained simple models by changing weight and bias.
